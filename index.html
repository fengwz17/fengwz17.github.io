About Me

I’m a Ph.D student at the Verification of System Software Group in the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS), supervised by Prof. Lijun Zhang and Prof. Zhilin Wu. I previously earned a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology at the School of EECS, Peking University.

My research interest includes Formal Verification for Software and Hardware Programs, and Large Language Model assisted Formal Methods.
